About Us

About Trailblazers

We are based in the beautiful City of Wada, attracting members from all over the India. We caters for a wide variety of cycling rides. our members are like-minded cyclists who share a passion for cycling and social engagement. We focus mainly on road cycling but We also have members who regualrly ride off-road. Above all we want everyone to enjoy riding their bikes as much as we do.

Our Activitis

Throughout the year we organise rides that explore the countryside around wada. If you want to join us on these breathtaking rides. Drop us a call to let us know that you are interested. Check out our activitis on Facebook, Instagram and Strava
